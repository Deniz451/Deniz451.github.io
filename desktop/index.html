Descent

About:

Descent is my Unity 2d game. It’s a top-down arcade game. The premise of the game goes like this: You are an angel sent from Heaven to Hell and you need to fight you way through and "descenet" the 9 circles of hell to defeat satan.

Gameplay:

The gameplay is quite simple. You are thrown into infinite arena and need to kill a certain number of enemies that spwan around you to trigger a boss. Once the boss is defeated you go to the next level. Rinse and repeat 9 times and you just finished the game. Each time you defeat a boss you gain a new abillity. You can also gain new upgrades to your weapons. A large arsenal of toys that will be your main line of defence against the demonic enemies.

Asteroids

About:

Asteroids, is my second Unity 2d project, that I am currently working on. Yet again it is an arcade game, set in space. You take controll of a small spaceship and need to navigate through big and dangerous asteroids fields. The game has little to no story and gameplay is completly infinite. In this project i really tried to recreate the nostalgic fell from the arcade games from the 80s.

Gameplay:

After you press play from the main menu the game starts right away. You are througn into the first level. After you manage to pass the asteroid field, you can either go to the next level or save your ptogress and quit. Each level gets progressivly harder (longer asteroid field), but you also get more poweups, like heal, dash, ammo and more.

Jump King with Guns

About:

This project is mine and my friends students project for school. We haven’t started working on it yet, but we already know some basics and are exited to start working on it in the next school year. The game itslef is, how you could have already guesst, yet again an arcade game, but now with a little bit more deapth. You play as one of 4 main heros that you can choose and you need to scale a tower all the way to the top.

Gameplay:

As i already mentioned, the primary goal of the game is scale the big tower all the way to the top. You can pick one of 4 playable characters, each with their own weapons and specializations. You start at the ground level and need to go up the tower. This task will make a bit more difficult the variety of enemies and bosses. The game is arcade/rogue-like so when you die you need to start all the way from the bottom but you get to keep some loot.
