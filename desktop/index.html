Descent

About:

Descent is my Unity 2d game. It’s a top-down arcade game. The premise of the game goes like this: You are an angel sent from Heaven to Hell and you need to fight you way through and "descenet" the 9 circles of hell to defeat satan.

Gameplay:

The gameplay is quite simple. You are thrown into infinite arena and need to kill a certain number of enemies that spwan around you to trigger a boss. Once the boss is defeated you go to the next level. Rinse and repeat 9 times and you just finished the game. Each time you defeat a boss you gain a new abillity. You can also gain new upgrades to your weapons. A large arsenal of toys that will be your main line of defence against the demonic enemies.
